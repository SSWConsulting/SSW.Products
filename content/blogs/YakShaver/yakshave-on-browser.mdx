Spotted a bug but don't have YakShaver installed? No problem. With **YakShave on Browser**, you can now shave issues directly from your browser — no desktop app, no extension, nothing to install!

![](/YakShaver/Blogs/Blogs/yakshave-now-button.png)
Figure: Find the "New Shave" button

## How it works

1. Click on the right top "New Shave" button, select "Shave in browser"
2. Pick your project and hit **Record** — choose the screen, window, or tab you want to share, and a short countdown gives you time to get ready
3. Show the problem and talk through it, just like you would explain it to a teammate
4. Optionally add written notes for extra context, then submit

From there, the AI agent takes over. It analyses your recording, follows your project's skill (your team's instructions for how work items should be written), and drafts the work item for you. Best of all, you can **watch the agent work live** — investigating, reasoning, and writing in real time, right in your browser.

## Why you'll love it

* **Zero setup** — works on macOS, Windows, and Linux in any modern browser
* **More ways to capture** — record your screen, film with your camera, or upload an existing video
* **Work items done right** — the agent follows your project's skill, so every item lands in your backlog meeting your team's standards

## Ready to shave?

Log in at [yakshaver.ai](https://yakshaver.ai), open your project, and hit **Record**. Your next bug report is one recording away. 🐂
