### Prerequisites
- You must be an Admin of your tenant in [YakShaver](https://portal.yakshaver.ai/)
- The external user must be invited as a [guest to your Microsoft Organization](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/communicate-with-users-from-other-organizations#guest-access)

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Log in to the [YakShaver Portal](https://portal.yakshaver.ai/)
2. In the [Users settings](https://portal.yakshaver.ai/settings/users), select **Show all users**
3. Activate the external user
4. Done!
