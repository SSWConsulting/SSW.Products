Project iterations let you break down the time tracked on a single project in greater detail. This makes it easy to provide your clients with insights such as: *How much time was spent on a specific milestone? How much time is expensed versus capitalized?*

Below, you’ll find steps to enable this feature for your projects and use it on your timesheets.
