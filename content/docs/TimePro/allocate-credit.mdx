When you need to reduce what a client owes on an invoice (for example adjustments, returns, or goodwill), TimePro allows you to **Add a Credit Note**.

Use this option when you need to formally reduce what a client owes, either against a specific invoice or at the client level for later allocation. 

> **Note:** Credit notes are the only available method for invoices in **locked periods**.

You can create a credit note in two ways:

## Option A: At the Invoice level

On an invoice, use the "Add Credit Note" button when you want to apply a credit directly against that invoice.

![](/TimePro/add-credit-note-invoice.png)

**Figure: Invoice - Use “add credit note”**

## Option B: At the Client level

On a client's page, use the “Create credit note” button to create a credit note outside of a specific invoice, when you want the credit recorded at the client level first, then allocated later if required.

![](/TimePro/add-credit-note-client.png)

**Figure: Client - Use “add credit note”**
