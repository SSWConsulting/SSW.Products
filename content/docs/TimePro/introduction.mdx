## üïë Smarter timesheets and effortless invoicing

[TimePRO](https://sswtimepro.com/) is a modern web-based **time tracking** and **invoicing** platform that helps teams log time quickly, invoice clients accurately, and connect with the tools they already use. No installation, no paperwork - just sign in and get to work. It focuses on:

* **Fast, consistent timesheeting** - Get timesheets done in seconds, not minutes.
* **Streamlined invoicing and receipting** - From captured time to client invoices with minimal friction.
* **One place for time and money** - Timesheets, invoices, expenses, and reporting in a single app.

TimePro is built as a **modern alternative** to tools like **Harvest**, with deeper integrations, more control over your data, and a workflow tuned for professional services teams.

## üöÄ Key Features

* **Quick timesheet entry** - Optimized for fast, daily time entry so developers and consultants can stay accurate without the overhead.
* **Azure DevOps integration** - Pull in work items, commits, and check-ins to pre-fill or suggest timesheet entries.
* **Xero sync** - Push invoices and receipts to Xero for accounting and compliance.
* **CRM integration** - Keep clients and projects in sync with Microsoft Dynamics 365 for invoicing and project tracking.
* **Prepaid and credit handling** - Support for prepaid packages, discounts, and credit notes so invoicing matches how you sell.
* **Multi-tenant** - One platform, many organizations; each tenant gets isolated data and configuration.
* **Modern stack** - .NET 8, Angular, SQL Server, and Azure-hosted for performance and maintainability.
* **EasyLeave** - Request leave, get approvals, and have leave time and calendar events updated automatically.
* **Reporting and Power BI** - Standard reports plus the flexibility to build your own views and dashboards.
